About Me

My Story

Hi! I am a UX Designer based in Massachusetts. I double-majored in Sociology and Spanish for the Professions, with a concentration in Global Studies at Worcester State University. My background in the humanities has allowed me to take a user-centric approach right from the get-go. I strive to create my best work and learn new concepts with each project, and tend to see improbabilities not as a limitation, but as an opportunity. My creative approach—no matter the medium—is to find points of tension, research them and discover creative solutions to implement into my designs that will satisfy the user. I do several rounds of user interviews and usability tests throughout my entire design process, and make iterations on each point based on this valuable feedback received from users. When I’m not designing, you can find me thriving in the unpredictable New England weather. I love hiking, snowboarding and adventuring. If I’m not out exploring, you can catch me reading a good book, or hanging out in my favorite bookstores. I am currently looking for Junior UX/UI opportunities in the Boston, MA area.
